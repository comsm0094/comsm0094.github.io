COMSM0094 - useful references and links

Books

There is no set reading list or textbook for this unit, this is a list of interesting and useful books relevant to the material.

  • Dayan and Abbott. The `classic' textbook: Theoretical Neuroscience: Computational and Mathematical Modeling of Neural Systems by Laurence F. Abbott and Peter Dayan
    • publishers page: mitpress.mit.edu
    • pdf: gatsby.ucl.ac.uk
  • Trappenberg. A nice and very popular book: Fundamentals of Computational Neuroscience by Thomas Trappenberg
  • Spikes. An interesting book which focusses on neuronal signalling: Spikes Exploring the Neural Code by William Bialek, Rob de Ruyter van Steveninck, Fred Rieke and David Warland.
      • publishers page: mitpress.mit.edu
  • The stike, this isn't a textbook but to avoid confusion with the above and because it is a great book: The spike: an epic journey though the brain in 2.1 seconds by Mark Humphries.
